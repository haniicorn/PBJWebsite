About Me

Hi! My name is Hannah, and I'm a sophomore at Babson College from Sharon, MA. I enjoy painting pretty pictures, listening to good music, and chasing sunrises. I love hanging out with my friends and eating good food around Boston.

At Babson College, I am a part of the Delta Sigma Pi (Δ∑∏) business fraternity, Babson Asian Pacific Student Association, and Product, Building, and Jamming. I chose Babson because I wanted to build businesses alongside my interpersonal and professional skillsets.

My hobbies include creating various kinds of arts and crafts projects. I like starting new creative pieces when I'm bored, and as presents for my friends. My favorite artist is Claude Monet, and his Water Lillies series. In his words, "The richness I achieve comes from nature, the source of my inspiration. "

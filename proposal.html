Project Proposal: PB&J Website

About:

PB&J Website is the home webpage for the Product, Building, & Jamming Club at Babson, showcasing our events, projects, members, and resources for students who want an into to the tech industry.

Objective:

To inform people about the Product, Building, & Jamming club and its activities.

Target Audience:

Babson students who do not know what PB&J club is, and/or Babson students who want to break into tech but do not know how to start.

Prospective Content:

  • Homepage
    • landing page, social media links, mission statement
  • Sitemap
    • links to other parts of website and social medias
  • Contact
    • contact/feedback form with email link
  • About
    • about the club, cohorts, members, eboard
  • Events Calendar
    • List of upcoming events
  • Project Gallery
    • photodump of past cohort projects
  • Resources
    • link to resources for people breaking into tech
